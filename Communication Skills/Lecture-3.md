# Adverbs
### Adverb of Manner
He runs <u>quickly</u>. 

### Adverb of place
He is playing <u>outside</u>.
### Adverb of Time
Soon, after, tomorrow, tonight etc
### Adverb of frequency
- Never
- Always
- Often
He <u>always</u> arrives on time
They <u>rarely</u> go out.

### Adverb of Degree
The movie was <u>very</u> entertaining

### Adverb of Certainty
They will <u>probably</u> come to the party.
